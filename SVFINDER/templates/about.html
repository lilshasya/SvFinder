About SvFinder

SvFinder is an innovative student-supervisor matching platform crafted to revolutionize the Final Year Project (FYP) experience at Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM).

Designed with the needs of both students and supervisors in mind, SvFinder empowers students to discover and connect with potential supervisors based on aligned research interests and academic goals.

Supervisors can effortlessly manage incoming applications, maintain balanced supervision loads, and monitor allocations with clarity and ease.

Our mission is simple yet impactful: to streamline and elevate the FYP supervision process through a centralized, intuitive system. With real-time updates and seamless communication, SvFinder ensures smoother collaborations and stronger academic partnerships.

This system was proudly conceived, developed, and brought to life by Syasya Fadhilah — an IT student with a vision to simplify and enrich the academic workflow at UKM.
